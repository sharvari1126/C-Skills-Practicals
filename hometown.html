Nagpur — The Orange City

Nagpur is a beautiful city in the center of India. It's known for its tasty oranges, clean streets, and peaceful vibes. It’s a mix of tradition, nature, and modern life.

Places to See

From lakes to forts, Nagpur has a lot to explore. Some places are quiet and calm, while others are full of energy and people.

Cool Things About Nagpur

  • It’s called the heart of India because it’s at the center of the country.
  • Very clean and peaceful, perfect for walks and chill evenings.
  • Famous for juicy oranges, especially in winter!
  • There are tiger reserves nearby for nature lovers.

Plan your visit today and experience the beauty of Nagpur yourself!
